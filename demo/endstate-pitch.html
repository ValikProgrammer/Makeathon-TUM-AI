The problem

B2B outbound is broken.

<1%
Cold email response rate. LinkedIn follows the same curve.
30 min
Research + first-touch qualification per lead. Doesn't scale.
§7 UWG
Cold-calling regulated. One careless campaign = abmahnung.
Meanwhile, buyers publish intent constantly — tenders, building permits, sustainability reports, job posts — that no one converts into timely, relevant outreach.
Our solution

Three agents. One purpose.

Scout

Only act on signals

Watches TED, DNK, public tenders 24/7. Creates a lead only with documented intent — never from a bought list.

Caller

Voice with consent

AI voice agent discloses itself, references the signal as the reason to call, qualifies 6 fields, asks permission to follow up.

Closer

Deliver only on opt-in

Sends the tailored offer via email or WhatsApp — only if the prospect explicitly asked for it. Escalates edge cases to a human.

Signal → Voice → Permission → Offer → Human only on escalation
